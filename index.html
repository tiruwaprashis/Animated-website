About बैकुण्ठधाम

बैकुण्ठधाम is a sacred place where families come to offer पिण्ड, a ritual performed for the peace of the departed souls. The eternal significance of these rites has been passed down through generations, symbolizing the journey from the physical world to the spiritual realm.

At बैकुण्ठधाम, the tranquil surroundings, flowing river, and traditional rituals offer a serene environment for families to honor their ancestors. Our services are designed to guide you through each step of the rituals, ensuring they are performed with the utmost respect and devotion.

We believe in preserving these time-honored traditions, helping future generations understand the importance of the spiritual journey and offering eternal peace to their loved ones.

Our Sacred Services

Pinda Daan (पिण्डदान)

Offering पिण्ड is a sacred ritual performed to honor ancestors and ensure their peace in the afterlife.

Antim Sanskar (अन्तिम संस्कार)

We provide full guidance in performing traditional funeral rites to ensure the soul's journey to peace.

Shradh (श्राद्ध)

The श्राद्ध ceremony is performed annually in memory of ancestors, invoking blessings and peace for the family.
